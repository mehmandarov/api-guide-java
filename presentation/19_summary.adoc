==== 5 Patterns Recap

[cols="1,3,4"]
|===
| # | Pattern | The Common Thread

| 1 | *Gatekeepers* | Filters clean input before business logic sees it
| 2 | *Security Shield* | JWT + RBAC with annotations, not if-statements
| 3 | *The Lens* | Enrich traces with domain context, not just HTTP metadata
| 4 | *Living Contract* | Generate OpenAPI from code, never maintain it separately
| 5 | *Evolution* | Version with URI + headers, using filters to route
| ⭐ | *Error Handling* | RFC 9457 Problem Details via `ExceptionMapper`
|===

*Filters and Mappers* are the backbone. They separate infrastructure from business logic.

* *All standard APIs* — Jakarta EE 11 + MicroProfile 7
* *Zero vendor lock-in* — same artifact deploys on Quarkus, Liberty, Helidon
* *74 tests* — runtime-agnostic, Testcontainers-based
